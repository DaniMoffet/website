HISTORY OF THE BATAVIA

Batavia left Texel, Holland on her maiden voyage to the exotic East Indies as the flagship of the Dutch East India Company fleet of 1629. She was commanded by one of the most experienced merchants, Francisco Pelsaert, but not even he could have foreseen what was in store for the Batavia and her crew...
